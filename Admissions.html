Admission Requirements

  • Completed Application Form with accurate personal information
  • Two recent passport-size photographs (colored, white background)
  • Clear copy of National ID or Passport (for international students)
  • Certified copies of High School Transcripts and Certificates
  • Valid Certificate of Good Conduct issued within the last 6 months
  • Proof of application fee payment (non-refundable)
  • Medical examination report from a recognized health facility

Registration Process

  1. Fill out the online application form completely
  2. Upload all required documents in the specified formats
  3. Pay the non-refundable application fee through available payment methods
  4. Submit your application and await confirmation email
  5. Check your application status regularly through your portal account
