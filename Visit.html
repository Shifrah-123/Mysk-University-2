Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about visiting Mysk University.

How do I schedule a campus tour?

You can schedule a campus tour by filling out the form on this page, calling our admissions office at +256 41 4 123 456, or emailing visit@mysk.edu.ug. We recommend scheduling at least one week in advance.

What should I bring for my campus visit?

We recommend bringing comfortable walking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, a notebook for any questions or observations, and a form of identification. If you're interested in specific programs, you might want to bring questions for faculty in those departments.

Can I meet with faculty during my visit?

Yes, we can arrange meetings with faculty members in your area of interest. Please indicate this preference when scheduling your visit, and we will do our best to coordinate based on faculty availability.

Is parking available on campus?

Yes, we provide complimentary parking for visitors in designated areas. When you schedule your visit, we will provide detailed parking instructions and a campus map.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my visit?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your campus visit, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance by calling +256 41 4 123 456 or emailing visit@mysk.edu.ug. We understand that plans can change and will be happy to reschedule your visit.
